Te Whare Tapa Whā; Sam's Wellbeing Plan

2024/04/08 | Week Four of Dev Academy | by Sam Whitmore
Wall Activities
Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing)
  • Swim.
  • Sauna.
  • Eat Well.
  • Sleep Well.
Taha Whānau (Family Wellbeing)
  • Attend family events.
  • Visit grandparents.
  • Contact immediate family.
  • Share my accomplishments.
Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing)
  • Practice; read philosophy.
  • Create and enjoy down-time.
  • Check in on my friends.
  • Document the profound ideas that pass by.
Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing)
  • Practice philosophy.
  • Reflect upon my progress.
  • Vary the scope of my perspective.
  • Regularly shift the incline of my learning curve.
Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment)
  • Walk outside.
  • Open windows.
  • Water plants and mow lawn.
  • Go to the beach.
  • Enjoy sunshine.
